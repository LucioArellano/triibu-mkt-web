SMS Terms and Conditions

SECTION 1 – Program Description

1.1 SMS Sent in Response to a Request

Triibu Marketing LLC operates an SMS communication program strictly limited to individuals who voluntarily submit their contact information through authorized digital channels.

These authorized channels include:

  • The official website of Triibu Marketing LLC
  • Landing pages operated and controlled by Triibu Marketing LLC
  • Online digital forms
  • Meta (Facebook and Instagram) Instant Lead Forms
  • Paid digital advertising campaigns managed by Triibu Marketing LLC

SMS messages are sent only after an individual:

  1. Voluntarily provides their phone number;
  2. Submits a request for information;
  3. Completes a valid opt-in process;
  4. Expressly consents to being contacted.

Triibu Marketing LLC does not:

  • Send unsolicited SMS messages;
  • Send cold messages to purchased or third-party lists;
  • Engage in automated bulk messaging without prior opt-in;
  • Contact individuals who have not directly provided their information.

All SMS communications are initiated in response to a user-driven action.

1.2 Purpose: Follow-Up and Appointment Scheduling

SMS communications are sent exclusively for purposes directly related to the inquiry submitted by the user. These purposes may include:

  • Responding to a vehicle or service inquiry;
  • Providing requested product or service details;
  • Coordinating appointment availability;
  • Confirming scheduled appointments;
  • Sending appointment reminders;
  • Providing updates regarding the submitted request;
  • Clarifying information related to the inquiry.

Triibu Marketing LLC operates as a marketing and lead generation agency.

When an individual submits a request regarding a specific product or service, Triibu Marketing LLC may contact that individual directly or as an authorized marketing representative of the business about which the inquiry was made.

Such businesses may include automotive dealerships, service providers, or other commercial entities promoted through our advertising campaigns.

Triibu Marketing LLC facilitates communication but does not act as the seller of the product or service.

All SMS communications are directly related to the original inquiry submitted by the user.

SECTION 2 – Consent

2.1 Consent Granted Upon Submission of Information

By submitting their phone number through an authorized digital form, landing page, or advertising lead form, the user provides express written consent to receive SMS communications from Triibu Marketing LLC.

Consent is obtained at the time of form submission and is recorded through the digital platform used to capture the lead.

The opt-in process requires the user to voluntarily provide their contact information in response to a clear request for information.

2.2 Not a Condition of Purchase

Providing consent to receive SMS communications is not a condition of purchasing any product or service.

Users are not required to agree to receive SMS messages in order to:

  • Purchase a vehicle;
  • Request a service estimate;
  • Conduct business with a dealership;
  • Complete any transaction.

Refusal to consent to SMS messaging will not result in denial of service.

2.3 Messages Sent Only to Users with Valid Opt-In

Triibu Marketing LLC sends SMS messages only to individuals who have completed a valid opt-in process.

We do not:

  • Use purchased contact lists;
  • Rent third-party databases;
  • Acquire phone numbers from external lead brokers;
  • Send SMS communications without direct consent.

All recipients of SMS messages have voluntarily submitted their information through an authorized opt-in mechanism.

SECTION 3 – Message Frequency

3.1 Message Frequency May Vary

Message frequency varies depending on:

  • The nature of the inquiry;
  • The stage of the appointment scheduling process;
  • The need for follow-up communication;
  • User interaction.

Users may receive multiple SMS messages related strictly to their submitted request.

Triibu Marketing LLC does not send excessive, repetitive, or unrelated SMS communications.

SECTION 5 – Cancellation

5.1 Reply STOP to Unsubscribe

Users may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying:

STOP

to any SMS message received from Triibu Marketing LLC.

This opt-out method is available at all times.

5.2 Processing Time for Opt-Out

After replying STOP:

  • The user may receive one final confirmation message;
  • The opt-out request will be processed promptly;
  • No further SMS communications will be sent unless new consent is provided.

If a user re-submits a new inquiry in the future, a new opt-in process may apply.
